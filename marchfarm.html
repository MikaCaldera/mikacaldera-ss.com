March Farms 

March Farm is a fourth generations family farm. It was an honor to work on their brand, website and digital brief. This was a site that would have been built in the early 2000s and the original logo was created by one of the March family members.  The members were not only a lot of fun to work with they also took a great deal of bride in their farm and what they produce.  This is one of my favorite projects of 2018. 

Brand & Design won the CADC - Excellence Award in 2018
Live Site: marchfarm.com
