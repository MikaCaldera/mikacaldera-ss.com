Riverside Church 

Riverside Church is a Christian church in Morningside Heights, Upper Manhattan, New York City. It opened its doors on October 5, 1930. The brand for this project was created by Jesse Hoyos, a great developer and designer.  His great work made it easy to use the brand in many different medias. 

The brand and colors being strong it made my job easy. My part in this project was mainly on their website revamping, which is now live. As well as email templates, social media, On the price side I helped with letterheads, business cards and flyers.
